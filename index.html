About Skardu – The Paradise of Northern Pakistan

Skardu is one of the most breathtaking tourist destinations in northern Pakistan. Surrounded by majestic mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and scenic valleys, it is a dream place for nature lovers and travelers. Every year, thousands of tourists visit Skardu to enjoy its peaceful environment, cool weather, and stunning landscapes.

Skardu is also known as the gateway to the world’s highest peaks, including K2 and the Karakoram mountain range. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and rich local culture, making it an unforgettable destination for families, photographers, and explorers.

Beautiful Places to Visit in Skardu

Skardu is home to many world-famous attractions such as Shangrila Resort, Upper Kachura Lake, Lower Kachura Lake, Deosai Plains, and Satpara Lake. These locations are known for their natural charm, peaceful atmosphere, and breathtaking views.

Deosai Plains, also called the “Land of Giants,” is one of the highest plateaus in the world. During summer, it is covered with colorful flowers and offers stunning views of wildlife, green fields, and snow-covered mountains.

Adventure & Trekking in Skardu

Skardu is a top destination for adventure tourism. Visitors come here for trekking, hiking, camping, jeep safaris, and mountain exploration. One of the most exciting activities is rock climbing, which attracts climbers from around the world who want to experience the thrill of climbing in the Karakoram mountains.

Professional tour companies provide complete travel packages, including trekking tours, cultural tours, and customized trips. These services help tourists explore glaciers, hidden valleys, and high-altitude landscapes safely and comfortably.

Whether you are an adventure seeker or a nature photographer, Skardu offers endless opportunities to enjoy and capture the beauty of mountains, lakes, and scenic skies.
